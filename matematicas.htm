Inspired by the success of the math in Spanish seminar at UNL, Laila and I are organizing a Spanish math seminar here at CSU!

El seminario de las matemáticas en español is a seminar for giving math talks in Spanish. All levels of Spanish proficiency are welcome! Recreational or low-level math talks are encouraged so as to be welcoming to people of all mathematical interests. The seminar is organized my myself and Laila Kettinger. This semester, we meet biweekly on Wednesdays at 4:00. Here are the talks given so far.
